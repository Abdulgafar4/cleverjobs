## Employer Design Flow

### 1) Entry & Onboarding
- Role selection → Sign up/sign in.
- Company profile basics: name, website, industry, location.
- Invite teammates (optional) → roles/permissions.
- Success screen → go to Employer Dashboard.

### 2) Employer Dashboard
- Overview cards: open jobs, new applicants, pipeline health.
- Quick actions: Post a job, Invite teammate, Connect integrations.

### 3) Post a Job
- JD assistant (AI) or template → title, description, requirements.
- Compensation, type, location/remote, visa, benefits.
- Preview → publish; option to boost/feature.
- Post‑publish: share links, copy to social, set alerts.

### 4) Applicants & Pipeline
- Job list → select job → Applicants tab.
- Pipeline (Kanban): New → Screen → Interview → Offer → Hired/Rejected (custom).
- Candidate profile: resume, parsed details, activity, notes, tags, history.
- Actions: advance, reject, bulk email, schedule interview, assign owner.

### 5) Scheduling & Communication
- Create interview: time slots, panelists, format.
- Calendar sync (Google/Microsoft), email invites, reminders.
- Message templates: rejection, next steps, logistics.

### 6) Sourcing & Matching
- Smart ranking (rule‑based/AI) vs JD; explain matched skills.
- Talent recommendations from pool; save to talent lists.
- Search & filters: skills, experience, location, tags.

### 7) Analytics & Reporting
- Funnel: views → applies → interviews → offers → hires.
- Source attribution & ROI; time‑to‑fill; stage conversion.
- Export CSV; scheduled email reports.

### 8) Settings & Integrations
- Company profile & brand page (media, benefits).
- Roles & permissions; audit trails.
- Integrations: ATS (future), calendar, email; API keys.

### Key States & Guards
- Unverified company → limit publishing until verified (optional).
- Not integrated calendars → show scheduling setup prompt.
- No applicants → nudge to refine JD or boost job.

### IA & Navigation
- Top nav (employer): Jobs, Applicants, Pipeline, Analytics, Settings.
- Job detail tabs: Overview, Applicants, Settings.
