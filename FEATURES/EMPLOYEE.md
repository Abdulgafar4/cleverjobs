## Employer Features

### Job Publishing
- **JD assistant (AI)**: Draft/refine job descriptions and required skills.
- **Boosted/featured jobs**: Sponsored placement for higher visibility.
- **Salary insights**: Market range recommendations and competitiveness score.

### Candidate Management
- **Applicant pipeline (Kanban)**: Stages, drag‑and‑drop, bulk actions.
- **Candidate profiles**: Parsed resumes, activity timeline, notes, tags.
- **Team collaboration**: Roles/permissions for recruiters and hiring managers.

### Sourcing & Matching
- **Smart candidate ranking (AI)**: Score applicants vs JD.
- **Talent recommendations**: Suggested candidates from the broader pool.
- **Talent pools**: Save, tag, nurture candidates; search & filter.

### Scheduling & Communication
- **Interview scheduling**: Time slots, calendar sync (Google/Microsoft), invites.
- **Message templates**: Rejections, next steps, interview logistics.
- **Automated reminders**: No‑show reduction for both sides.

### Analytics & Reporting
- **Funnel analytics**: Views → applies → interviews → offers → hires.
- **Source attribution**: Channels performance and ROI.
- **Compliance reporting**: EEOC, consent logs, data retention.

### Integrations
- **ATS integrations**: Greenhouse/Lever (import/export, webhooks).
- **Calendar & email**: Scheduling and communication sync.
- **SSO & HRIS (future)**: Identity and onboarding data flows.

### Brand & Community
- **Company profile**: Rich page with culture, benefits, and media.
- **Followers & updates**: Announce new roles to followers.
- **Referral program**: Track and reward referrals.

### Security & Privacy
- **Access controls**: Fine‑grained permissions and audit trails.
- **Data policies**: GDPR/CCPA tools for candidate data requests.

### Future (Big Bets)
- **Assessment suite**: Skill tests with proctoring basics.
- **Full scheduling suite**: Panel interviews, rooms, buffer policies.
- **Embedding-based matching**: Vector search for top‑K candidate/job matches.
