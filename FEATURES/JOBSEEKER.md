## Job Seeker Features

### Core
- **Personalized job feed (AI-ranked)**: Show jobs relevant to resume/profile.
- **Saved jobs & recently viewed**: Quick access to opportunities.
- **Saved searches & alerts**: Email/push notifications for new matches.
- **Robust filters**: Salary range, remote-only, visa sponsorship, type.
- **One‑click apply**: Autofill from stored profile/resume.
- **Application tracker**: Statuses, reminders, and personal notes.

### AI Assistance
- **AI cover letter generator**: Tailor to each job from resume + JD.
- **Resume quality checker**: ATS readiness, keyword coverage, readability.
- **Skill gap analysis**: Missing skills vs JD + upskilling suggestions.
- **Interview prep**: Practice Q&A from job description, STAR templates.

### Discovery & Research
- **Company follow & updates**: New jobs, news, and changes.
- **Salary insights**: Estimated ranges by title/location/seniority.
- **Job compare**: Side-by-side benefits, salary, and requirements.
- **Recommendations**: “Because you viewed/applied…” carousels.

### Profile & Resume
- **Resume upload & parsing**: Extract skills, experience, education.
- **Profile builder**: Title, skills, links, location, preferences.
- **Multiple resumes**: Choose a tailored version per application.

### Notifications
- **In‑app notifications**: Applications, messages, interview requests.
- **Email & web push**: Opt‑in alerts for activity and new matches.

### Accessibility & UX
- **PWA support**: Installable app, quick access.
- **Internationalization**: Locale-aware formatting and content.
- **Privacy controls**: Profile visibility and data export/delete.

### Future (Big Bets)
- **Vector search matching**: Embedding-based job similarity.
- **Integrated assessments**: Short skill checks with feedback.
- **Referral tracking**: Apply with referral links and bonuses.
